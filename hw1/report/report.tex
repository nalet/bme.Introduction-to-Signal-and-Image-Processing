where the vertices meet is $360$, also point $b$. By that circumstances we can use
\[ \frac{180(a-2)}{a}b=360 \]
In a simple matter this leads us to
\[ (a-2)b=2a \]

\[ a=-2,b=1;a=1,b=-2,a=3,b=6;a=4,b=4;a=6,b=3 \]
